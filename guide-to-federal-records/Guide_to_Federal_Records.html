Guide to Federal Records in the National Archives of the United States

The Web Version of the Guide

This web version of the Guide to Federal Records in the National Archives of the United States supersedes a paper version with the same title compiled by Robert B. Matchette et al in 1995, and the previous web version based on that text. The current Guide is derived from live data directly from the National Archives Catalog instead.

This version incorporates descriptive information about newly acquired federal records as soon as the relevant catalog records have been updated.
